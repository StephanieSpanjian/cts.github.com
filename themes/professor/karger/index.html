I am a member of the Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence Laboratory in the EECS department at MIT. My interests include information retrieval (particularly our haystack project) and analysis of algorithms. I have also spent some time working at Akamai and consulting for Google and Vanu Inc. Last year I spent half my time on sabbatical at MSR New England.

My resume is here.

This semester I am teaching 6854: Advanced Algorithms. I often teach 6.856: Randomized Algorithms. I also teach a dance class for PE credit
